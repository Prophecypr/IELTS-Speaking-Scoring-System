        万能短语

        直接回答 + 展开 + 对比/举例

        Yes/No, + [直接给出观点]...
        Actually / To be honest, + [补充理由]...
        For example / For instance, + [举具体例子]...
        (Optional) But I suppose + [转折或对比]...
        "Do you like reading?"
        Yes, I actually enjoy reading quite a lot, especially novels. To be honest, it's one of my favourite ways to unwind after a long day. For instance, last week I finished a science fiction book and I found it absolutely fascinating.

        PEEL 结构（适合展开型问题）

        P — Point: 直接表明立场/观点
        E — Evidence: 给出原因/背景
        E — Example: 举具体例子
        L — Link: 总结/回扣问题

        Part 2 卡片题完整框架（1–2 分钟）

        I'd like to talk about [主题]...
        When: 时间/背景 → "This happened about [时间] ago / back when I was..."
        Where: 地点 → "It took place in / at [地点]..."
        Who/What: 细节 → "I was with [人物] / The [事物] was..."
        Why memorable / how you felt: "What made it special was... / I felt incredibly..."
        Conclusion: "Overall, it was [形容词] experience because..."
        "Describe a memorable journey."
        I'd like to talk about a trip I took to Guilin about two years ago. It took place during the National Day holiday, and I went with my university friends — there were four of us in total. What made it unforgettable was the breathtaking landscape, the karst mountains reflected in the Li River were unlike anything I'd ever seen. I felt genuinely awestruck. Overall, it was one of the most refreshing experiences I've had, mainly because it gave me a complete break from city life.

        观点表达模板（Part 3 讨论题）

        I think / I believe / In my view + [观点]...
        This is because / The main reason is + [理由]...
        Take [例子] as an example...
        On the other hand / However + [反面/让步]...
        So overall / All things considered + [总结立场]...

        比较分析模板

        Compared to [A], [B] tends to be more [形容词]...
        While [A] has the advantage of..., [B] is better in terms of...
        In the past, people used to... but nowadays...
        It depends on + [条件]. If..., then... If..., however...

        开场 & 过渡短语

        Well, that's an interesting question... / Let me think about that for a second...
        As far as I'm concerned... / From my perspective...
        Having said that... / That being said...
        What I mean is... / In other words...
        It's hard to generalise, but... / It really depends on...

        高分词汇替换

        good → excellent / outstanding / remarkable
        very → incredibly / remarkably / tremendously
        big → enormous / substantial / significant
        like → enjoy / be fond of / be passionate about
        think → believe / reckon / be of the opinion
        because → due to / owing to / as a result of

        导入题库 JSON

        支持导入自定义题库 JSON 文件，导入后题目将合并到现有题库中（相同 id 的题目不会被重复导入）。

        JSON 格式说明：
        • 数组格式：直接是一个数组 [...]
        • 对象格式：{"questions": [...]}

        单题字段说明：
        • id：唯一编号（数字，不可重复）
        • part：1、2 或 3
        • topic：话题名称（如 "Music"）
        • src：来源标签（如 "5-8月新题"、"1-4月题目"）
        • text：题目文本（Part 2 用 \n 分隔各部分）
        • questions：数组，Part 1 的各小问（可选）
        • p3：数组，Part 2 对应的 Part 3 讨论题（可选）
